How to Submit a Complaint

1. Select Department

Select the department related to your complaint.

2. Choose Category

Choose a category that best describes your issue.

3. Provide Description

Provide a detailed description of your complaint.

4. Enter Location

Enter your location for better assistance.

5. Upload Media

Optionally, upload any images or videos related to your complaint.

6. Submit

Click on the "Submit Complaint" button to send your complaint.
